Societal Impacts of Computer Science

The benefits of sharing information

  • The internet provides a wide variety of materials to be accessed across the world
  • No matter what topic you want to learn about, there will be content available on the internet for you to find
  • It provides a better education for children, if you have a question, it can most likely be answered in an instant with the availability of information
  • People can learn about how computers work:
    • This can be through programming
    • Building their own computer
    • Using websites like Hackerrank, Codecademy, TryHackMe to further their skills in their chosen area of interest
